# Lighting

## Fall-off

## Natural Light
  - High quality
  - Not consistent
  - Can be too sharp in full sun

## Continuous Light
  - Typically lower quality
  - Pricier bulbs can be consistent
  - Easy to control

## Strobes
  - Typically the highest quality
  - Very directional
  - More difficult to control

## Reflectors
  - Dirt cheap
  - Easy to use and control
